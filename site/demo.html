What Pensees does

1. Fuzzy idea

You arrive with a half-formed idea you cannot yet hand to anyone. Pensees stops, restates what it heard, and names the ambiguities (linguistic, intent, contextual, epistemic, interactional). No plan dump on turn one.

2. One question at a time

Each Pensees turn ends with at most one question mark. Multiple-choice options always include (d) none of these, let me describe and (e) I want option (X) detailed first - so you stay in control of the dialogue's depth.

3. Verifiable spec

When the 7-row convergence checklist is fully green and you reply with an explicit approval token, Pensees emits requirements.md and acceptance-criteria.md under .local/pensees/{date}-{slug}/outputs/. Anything else is forbidden by the HARD-GATE rule (F-14).

Install in 30 seconds

Recommended (curl) One line, no clone, no build step. 
curl -fsSL https://yorha-agents.github.io/Pensees/get.sh | sh

Dev fallback (uses bash, not sh):

curl -fsSL https://yorha-agents.github.io/Pensees/get.sh | bash

Verify the symlink landed:

ls "$HOME/.cursor/skills-cursor/pensees"
Alternative (git clone)

If you would rather hold a local checkout.

git clone https://github.com/YoRHa-Agents/Pensees.git
cd Pensees
./install.sh

Manual symlink fallback

When install.sh cannot reach your home directory.

mkdir -p "$HOME/.cursor/skills-cursor"
ln -snf "$PWD/skill" "$HOME/.cursor/skills-cursor/pensees"
mkdir -p "$HOME/.claude/skills"
ln -snf "$PWD/skill" "$HOME/.claude/skills/pensees"
mkdir -p "$HOME/.codex/skills"
ln -snf "$PWD/skill" "$HOME/.codex/skills/pensees"

Where output lands

Every session lives under one date-slug directory; nothing escapes it.

.local/
  pensees/
    INDEX.md
    .audit/
      destruction.log
    {YYYY-MM-DD}-{slug}/
      transcript.md
      ontology.yaml
      checklist-status.md
      demos/
        01-{topic}-A.html
        01-{topic}-B.html
      outputs/
        requirements.md
        acceptance-criteria.md
