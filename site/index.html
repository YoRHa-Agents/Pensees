Why this exists

  • Status-quo agents jump to a plan dump on the first vague prompt.
  • Pensees stops, names the ambiguity, and asks one structured question.
  • A 7-row checklist gates the final deliverable; no deliverable until the user explicitly approves.

Three steps

1. Fuzzy idea

"Help me design a small CLI for X" - ambiguity surfaced.

2. One question at a time

Structured multiple-choice with (d) none of these and (e) detail (X) first.

3. Verifiable spec

After 7 checklist rows turn ok and you approve, two files land in .local/pensees/.
